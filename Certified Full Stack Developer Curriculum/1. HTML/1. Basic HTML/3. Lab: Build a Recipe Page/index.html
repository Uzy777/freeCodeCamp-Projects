Nando's Chicken & Chips (Mild)

This delicious Nando's Chicken and Chips recipe is perfect for those who enjoy a mild spice level. With tender, marinated chicken and crispy chips, this dish is sure to please everyone at the table. Follow the simple steps below to recreate this popular dish at home!

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on)
  • 2 tablespoons Nando's mild peri-peri sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large potatoes (for chips)
  • Optional: Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix the chicken thighs with Nando's mild peri-peri sauce, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Ensure the chicken is well coated.
  3. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray and bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  4. While the chicken is baking, peel and cut the potatoes into thick chips.
  5. Boil the chips in salted water for about 5 minutes, then drain and let them dry.
  6. Heat oil in a frying pan and fry the chips until golden and crispy. Alternatively, you can bake them in the oven for a healthier option.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  8. Serve the chicken with the crispy chips and garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
